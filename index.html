The Future of Accessible Carbon Capture

Efficiently capturing carbon in accessible forms, through paint and paint additives.

Problem & Solution

  • The Problem
  • The Solution
  • How it works

33 billion tons of carbon dioxide is released into the air every year. Almost every sector transportation, electricity, and even agriculture produce carbon dioxide emissions. Carbon dioxide is one of the most released gases by human activities. Carbon dioxide is also a greenhouse gas that contributes wildly to climate change. Decreasing carbon dioxide emissions is a great first step to reducing climate change, but in order to completely stop it, we need to start to reduce the amount of carbon dioxide in the air by carbon capture.

CaptureCoat is the revolution of carbon dioxide capturing technology. Painters simply add CaptureCoat to their paint or use a paint with CaptureCoat already added, and paint like they normally would. When the paint dries, it’ll capture carbon from the atmosphere around it passively. No fancy implementation or mess. Just paint like normal, and CaptureCoat takes care of the environment on its own.

Our carbon capture technology utilizes the carbon transformation abilities of titanium dioxide nanoparticles, in order to break down carbon dioxide. The nanoparticle is mixed in with different paint like substances, and when on your walls, it takes in Co2, and breaks it down, clearing the air around you, but also cleaning the atmosphere.

Who are we?

Carbon capture has been the frontier of eco-tech for the last 10 years. But, even after so much development, the average person can’t get their hands on it. That’s where CaptureCoat comes in. We use nanotechnology in different paint solutions, to bring carbon capture to your home, and help you save the planet.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a cleaner and healthier environment for the future. Climate change effects include precipitation, droughts, melting ice, and many more. CaptureCoat will capture carbon from the air in order to reverse climate change and its detrimental effects to create a better environment on Earth.
